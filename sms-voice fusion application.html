SMS-Voice Fusion Application

Horizon Mobitech Private Limited has performed a detailed research on the usage of short code and other related SMS services. We have found out that whenever an interested person sends an SMS, and then he/ she would want to talk to an executive as soon as possible. Hence, we have developed a new innovative service that combines a short code and IVR outbound call.

  • Given below are the details of how this service works:
  • Prospective customers who are interested in your products & services, will message '' on short code 53030 and gets a text message as acknowledgment (FREE OF COST).
  • Along with this they also get an automated IVR coded voice call explaining about the services, support & payment related inquiries, etc.
  • Customers can select an option of their choice and can also be patched in real time with your marketing or support executives.

Application Benefits:

  • SMS short code & IVR call integration
  • Instant call conference of the customer and the executive, leading to immediate query resolution
  • Enhances brand strength by addressing to customer needs in a professional way
  • Responding to the customer in their desired language
  • Detailed reports would be provided, based on the call flow, such as the person able to talk to your executive, which executive handled a particular call, duration of conversation, etc.
