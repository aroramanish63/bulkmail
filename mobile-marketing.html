MOBILE MARKETING

The mobile marketing service group includes all kinds of SMS based marketing services. This includes one-way as well as two-way SMS based marketing services.

Focused SMS campaigns

We conduct focused SMS campaigns for clients with suggested target base segregation. We work with organization which conducts campaigns on legitimate non-DND data. All campaigns are conducted in line with the TRAI regulations & related DND restrictions.

Promotional messaging gateway

This is a web-based promotional messaging gateway through which multiple promotional SMS can be sent at a given point of time.

Transactional Messaging Gateway

This is a web-based transactional messaging gateway, though which messages "Transactional in nature" - can be sent to the desired set of customers

Two-way SMS Short code

SMS Short code service is commonly used for lead generation or receiving feedbacks from the prospective customers. This is known as a two-way short code because it includes the following two steps:

  • i. Customer requesting for information
  • ii. Organization replying to customer´s needs.

A Keyword is a unique identifier for an organization on the short code. For instance, if your company’s name is "ORG Ltd" and you took a keyword say "ORG Ltd" on our short code then all customers enquiring on about your organization through the short code will be published on a URL that we would provide you.

  • How does our short code service work?
  • Customer sends the SMSes the keyword on 53030. Other short codes such as (56677, 58888 can be made available on request)
  • A free automated reply content (customized) will be sent to that mobile number.
  • The details of the customer sending an SMS will be displayed on a URL as shared by us.
  • The details of the customer sending an SMS will be displayed on a URL as shared by us.

Application Benefits:

  • Can be used for lead generation purposes, customer query registration, advertising campaigns (on radio, TV and print media)
  • Can be integrated with client’s databases to provide real-time query-search integration.
  • Easy to remember contact method for customers

Opt-in Messaging Application

This is a specialized service developed, where a customer who wants to receive messages from a certain client, can "Opt-in" for receiving the messages. On doing this, the customer can receive any message from the respective client till the time he does not "Opt-out".

  • Given below is how this service works:
  • A virtual number is developed and the opt-in application is hosted on the same
  • Any customer who sends "START" on this virtual number will register to receive promotional messages from this organization
  • The organization can now send any promotional SMS to this customer and the message gets delivered through a sender id.
  • In case a customer wishes to opt-out of this service, then he just needs to send "STOP" on the same virtual number and will stop getting messages.
  • Benefits of this application:
  • Organizations can send promotional messages to customers who really want them and have opted in for them
  • ll messages are delivered through a characterized "Sender Id"
  • Higher impact and also the organization gets to know the customers who are really interested in their products and services.
